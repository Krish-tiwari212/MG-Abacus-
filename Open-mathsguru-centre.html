Why open an MG-Abacus Centre

We use mathematical concepts, as well as the skills we learn from doing math problem, every day. Maths is all around us and helps us understand the world better. MG-Abacus have build a unique program, using Soroban Abacus, to encourage kids to be learn and excel in maths.


With the proven system, it is easy to get started and open you own MG-Abacus centre and join the mission to spread the love of Maths.

Who is the opportunity for

This opportunity is for anyone who interested in generating extra income. You don’t need to be an Maths genius but should be passionate about Maths and interested in teaching.


Below are some of the traits that are handy for running the centre.

  • Enjoy interactions with students and parents
  • Skilled at marketing and business development
  • Resourceful and multi-tasking individuals
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills

What is the opportunity

MG-Abacus has a well established system to help you in every step. To start the MG-Abacus centre you don’t need a big investment or hire a venue. You will receive all the necessary training. You can start from your home and as the enrolment increase you can hire a venue to run the classes.
